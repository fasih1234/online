WELCOME!TO FBISE


FBISE officers are available for students,institutes and general public through online appointment,Email,Phone&Fax.
Controller of examinations deals with:
  • Matters pertaining to conduct examination
  • Establishment of examination centers and change of center/subject.
  • Preparation and issuance of date sheet ,roll number slips.
  • Issuance of material to examination centers.
  • Materrs relating to question papers.
  • Marking to answer scripts
  • Compilation of results
  • Re counting of answer scripts.
  • Issuance of mark sheets
  • UFM cases are dealt under the supervision of controller of Examinations.
